Member operator is also called a dot operator since its symbol is a dot (.).
This operator tells about the member of a package or class. It is used in three ways.

1. We can use . operator to access class of a package.
Syntax : packagename.classname
Eg : java.io.BufferedReader

2. To refer the variable of a class.
Syntax : objectname.variablename or classname.variablename
Eg : System.out 

3. To refer the method of a class.
Syntax : objectname.methodname or classname.methodname
Eg : Math.sqrt()
